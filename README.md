# RAFT TUTOR AXIS

India's Growing Teacher Network

## About

Raft Tutor Axis (RTA) is a trusted teacher-providing platform dedicated to connecting qualified educators with schools, institutes, and students across India.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible by providing skilled and verified teachers for:

- Home Tuition
- Online Classes
- School Teacher Recruitment
- Institute Faculty Hiring
- Competitive Exam Mentorship
- Language & Skill Training

## Features

- Teacher Registration
- Parent Registration
- School & Institute Solutions
- Home Tutor Matching
- Responsive Design
- Hindi & English Language Support
- Contact & Support System
